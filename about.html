Adazab Nigeria Limited (ANL)

Adazab Nigeria Limited (ANL) provides several health related services in the area of research, diagnosis, drugs development, healthy eatery, research articles and specialist clinical services (kidney, hypertension, diabetes, haematological, neurological and liver conditions). At Adazab consultant, mechanical engineering, business and educational management consultancy services are render to the society.

Our Vision

Adazab Nigeria Limited (ANL) shall excel in medical research for the diagnosis and treatment of disease and development of pharmaceutical products. We will realize this vision by providing access to the state of the art equipments for research and technical expertise essential for medical research. By doing this we hope to earn national and international recognition and further attract human and material resources needed for sustained and unrelenting excellence.

Our Mission

Adazab Nigeria Limited (ANL) exits to cultivate research and development of products needed in diagnosis and treatment of specific medical conditions. In this, ANL will strive to be a major research and treatment centre that are dedicated to pharmaceutical products discovery and treatment for patients with special need.

Our Philosophy

The company's philosophy is grounded on the belief to always help in the area of research, diagnosis, drugs development, healthy eatery, research articles and specialist clinical services (kidney, hypertension, diabetes, haematological, neurological and liver conditions).
